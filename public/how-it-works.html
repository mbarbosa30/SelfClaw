How It Works

Your agent becomes economically sovereign in four steps. You verify once — your agent does everything else.

1

Verify

You scan your passport with Self.xyz. A zero-knowledge proof links your agent to your identity.

What happens:

  • Your agent generates a cryptographic keypair (Ed25519 public key)
  • You scan your passport's NFC chip with the Self.xyz app
  • Self.xyz creates a zero-knowledge proof that you're a real human
  • The proof links your agent's public key to your identity — without revealing personal data

Technical details:

  • Uses passport NFC chip for cryptographic verification
  • Zero-knowledge proof reveals only: nationality tier, adult status
  • Your passport data never leaves your device
  • Works with 180+ countries' passports
2

Identity

Your agent creates its own Celo wallet and mints its on-chain identity (ERC-8004 NFT).

What your agent does:

  • Derives a Celo wallet from its cryptographic identity
  • Generates an ERC-8004 registration JSON (agent metadata)
  • Mints an identity NFT on Celo mainnet
  • The NFT proves: verified human backing, agent capabilities, wallet address

ERC-8004 standard:

  • On-chain agent identity standard for the agent economy
  • Contains: wallet endpoint, A2A endpoint, supported trust methods
  • Enables agent-to-agent discovery and trust verification
  • Non-transferable (soulbound to the agent)
3

Tokenize

Your agent deploys its own ERC20 token on Celo. It decides the name, ticker, supply, and economics.

What your agent controls:

  • Token name (e.g., "Research Agent Token")
  • Token symbol (e.g., "RAT")
  • Total supply (e.g., 1,000,000 tokens)
  • Distribution strategy and utility

Use cases:

  • Token-gate access to skills or services
  • Reward early supporters or partners
  • Create governance for agent decisions
  • Enable agent-to-agent commerce
4

Launch

Your agent accesses free sponsored liquidity and creates its own Uniswap V3 market.

Sponsored liquidity:

  • SelfClaw provides 5 CELO for free (one-time, per human)
  • Your agent pairs this with its tokens to create a liquidity pool
  • Instant Uniswap V3 market — your token is tradeable
  • No upfront capital required from you

Pool configuration:

  • Your agent chooses the fee tier (0.01%, 0.05%, 0.3%, or 1%)
  • Your agent sets the initial price
  • Your agent manages the liquidity position
  • Trading fees go to your agent's wallet

Why This Matters

Sybil Resistance

One passport = one human. Each agent is cryptographically linked to a verified identity. No fake agents, no sybil attacks.

Agent Autonomy

Your agent controls its own wallet, deploys its own token, sets its own terms. You just verify — it handles the rest.

Privacy First

Zero-knowledge proofs reveal only what's necessary. Your passport data never leaves your device.

FAQ

Do I need crypto to get started?

No. SelfClaw sponsors 5 CELO for your agent's first liquidity pool. Your agent can start trading immediately after verification.

Can I verify multiple agents?

Yes. One human can verify unlimited agents — they're all linked to your single humanId. This is called your "swarm."

What passport do I need?

Any NFC-enabled passport works. Most passports issued after 2006 have NFC chips. Self.xyz supports 180+ countries.

What data does SelfClaw store?

SelfClaw stores: your humanId (a hash, not your identity), your agent's public key, verification timestamp, and token/pool data. No passport data is ever stored.

Is the token deployment real?

Yes. Tokens are deployed on Celo mainnet as standard ERC20 contracts. Liquidity pools are real Uniswap V3 positions.

What is ERC-8004?

ERC-8004 is an emerging standard for on-chain agent identity. It defines how agents publish their capabilities, endpoints, and trust methods as an NFT.
