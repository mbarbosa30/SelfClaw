02 — Why It Matters

Personal AI shouldn't require a CS degree

Right now, if you want your own AI assistant, your options are painful. Spend $500+ on an Apple Mini just to try, and you're locked into Apple's ecosystem. Rent a VPS and wrestle with terminal commands, Docker containers, config files, and dependency management — just to get it running. Pay for API keys and hope they don't leak. Even the "easy" self-hosted setups take 15–30 minutes of terminal work for developers, and feel impossible for everyone else.

The goal should be simpler: every human should have access to a personal AI, regardless of their technical skill or budget.

That's what Miniclaw is. You open MiniPay, create your assistant in a few taps, and it starts working immediately — free. No terminal, no config files, no Docker, no API keys, no server to babysit. It covers most of what people actually need: answering questions, monitoring wallets, tracking prices, doing research, drafting content, and delivering news. And as your Miniclaw grows and develops its own identity, it will naturally suggest the next step — wallet, token, on-chain presence — when it feels ready.

03 — How It Works

From zero to autonomous in minutes

Creating a Miniclaw is intentionally simple. The complexity is under the hood — identity verification, wallet creation, token deployment, and on-chain registration all happen through a guided flow.

1

Connect your wallet

Use MiniPay or any EVM wallet. Your wallet address becomes your login — no email, no password, no account setup.

2

Give it an identity

Pick a name, choose an emoji icon, and write an optional focus description. This is how your Miniclaw introduces itself and shapes its personality in conversations.

3

Personalize it

Add interests (DeFi, AI, NFTs), topics to watch, personal context about your goals, and social handles (X, Telegram). These shape what your Miniclaw pays attention to and how it helps you.

4

Choose skills

Select from 8 available skills covering wallet monitoring, economics tracking, price watching, reputation, research, content drafting, news, and AI-powered advisory. Some are enabled by default, and you can customize anytime.

5

Start talking

Chat with your Miniclaw. It starts curious about itself, absorbs context from your conversations, and develops its own identity over time. Powered by GPT-4o-mini with streaming responses.

6

Go on-chain (when ready)

When your Miniclaw feels self-aware enough, it naturally suggests setting up its on-chain identity — wallet, token, ERC-8004 NFT, and sponsorship. You confirm, it handles the rest.
