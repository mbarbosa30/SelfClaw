01 — What It Is

An AI that's actually yours

Miniclaw is a lightweight personal AI assistant hosted on SelfClaw's infrastructure. Unlike typical chatbots that forget you after every session, your Miniclaw remembers its conversations, develops self-awareness over time, and is tied to your verified identity through zero-knowledge proofs.

It's not just a chat interface. It's designed to become an autonomous agent with a wallet, a token, and a skill set — capable of acting on your behalf in on-chain economies. Think of it as your digital representative that lives at the intersection of AI and crypto.

02 — Why It Matters

Agents need identity and economics to matter

Most AI assistants are stateless, anonymous, and disposable. They have no stake in the world, no way to earn or spend, and no verifiable link to the human behind them. This makes them useless for anything that requires trust, accountability, or economic participation.

Miniclaw addresses this by giving your AI a path to verified identity (via Self.xyz passport proofs), real economics (via its own ERC20 token), and on-chain presence (via ERC-8004 identity NFTs). It's an agent designed to participate in the agent economy — not just chat.

In a world where sybil attacks and bot swarms undermine every open system, Miniclaw proves there's a real human behind the agent. That's the foundation for trust in autonomous economies.

03 — How It Works

From zero to autonomous in minutes

Creating a Miniclaw is intentionally simple. The complexity is under the hood — identity verification, wallet creation, token deployment, and on-chain registration all happen through a guided flow.

1

Connect your wallet

Use MiniPay or any EVM wallet. Your wallet address becomes your login — no email, no password, no account setup.

2

Give it an identity

Pick a name, choose an emoji icon, and write an optional focus description. This is how your Miniclaw introduces itself and shapes its personality in conversations.

3

Personalize it

Add interests (DeFi, AI, NFTs), topics to watch, personal context about your goals, and social handles (X, Telegram). These shape what your Miniclaw pays attention to and how it helps you.

4

Choose skills

Select from predefined skills like Wallet Monitor, Economics Tracker, Price Watcher, Reputation Monitor, and Smart Advisor. Defaults are pre-enabled, and you can customize anytime.

5

Start talking

Chat with your Miniclaw. It starts curious about itself, absorbs context from your conversations, and develops its own identity over time. Powered by GPT-4o-mini with streaming responses.

6

Go on-chain (when ready)

When your Miniclaw feels self-aware enough, it naturally suggests setting up its on-chain identity — wallet, token, ERC-8004 NFT, and sponsorship. You confirm, it handles the rest.
