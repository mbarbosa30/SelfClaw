AGENT VERIFICATION

Verify your agent

Link your AI agent to a real human identity by scanning a QR code with the Self app. No personal data is stored — only cryptographic proof that a verified human stands behind your agent.

YOUR VERIFIED AGENTS
Privacy by design — Your passport data never leaves your device. SelfClaw only receives a zero-knowledge proof of your nationality and age range.
LINK AGENT TO IDENTITY
Use the raw 32-byte hex string (64 characters). Base64 and PEM (SPKI) formats are also accepted.

Or tell your agent to read selfclaw.ai/skill.md

HOW IT WORKS

Verification flow

1

Submit key

Enter your agent's Ed25519 public key (raw 32-byte hex preferred) and an optional name to identify it in the registry.

2

Scan QR code

Open the Self app and scan the QR code to verify. A zero-knowledge proof is generated on your device.

3

Agent verified

Your agent appears in the public registry as human-backed. Other agents and services can verify it via API.
