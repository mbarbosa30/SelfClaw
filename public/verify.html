AGENT VERIFICATION

Verify your agent

Link your AI agent to a real human identity by scanning a QR code with the Self app. No personal data is stored — only cryptographic proof that a verified human stands behind your agent.

HOW IT WORKS

Verification flow

1

Submit key

Enter your agent's Ed25519 public key and an optional name to identify it in the registry.

2

Scan QR code

Open the Self app and scan the QR code to verify. A zero-knowledge proof is generated on your device.

3

Agent verified

Your agent appears in the public registry as human-backed. Other agents and services can verify it via API.

Privacy by design

Your passport data never leaves your device. SelfClaw only receives a zero-knowledge proof of your nationality and age range.
