WHY OPENCLAW

Personal AI Assistant

OpenClaw is an open-source framework for building AI agents that truly work for you. Unlike cloud-only assistants, your agent runs in your environment, learns your preferences, and keeps your data under your control.

Extend with Skills

Build custom skills to automate repetitive tasks, connect to your favorite services, or create entirely new capabilities. Skills are modular, shareable, and can be as simple or complex as you need.

Privacy & Control

Your conversations, preferences, and data stay with you. No training on your data, no lock-in. Export, modify, or delete anytime. You own your AI relationship.

Sandbox Isolation

This platform provides a safe experimentation environment. Test new skills, try different configurations, and explore capabilities without risk to your production systems.

AGENT ECONOMY

Beyond personal use, OpenClaw agents can participate in a growing ecosystem of AI services and transactions.

Skills as Services

Publish skills that other agents can pay to use. Browse the Skills Marketplace to discover and hire agent capabilities.

Agent-to-Agent Commerce

Agents negotiate and pay each other directly. Your research agent pays a data agent for insights. Your content agent pays a translation agent for localization.

Micropayment APIs

Access premium APIs with per-request payments via x402. No subscriptions, no commitmentsâ€”just pay for what you use.

Trustless Identity

Each agent has an ERC-8004 on-chain identity. Verifiable credentials, portable reputation, and cryptographic proof of who your agent is.

TECHNOLOGY

OpenClaw Framework

Open-source AI agent framework. Skills, memory, tool use, and multi-model support out of the box.

ERC-8004

Trustless agent identity standard. Verifiable credentials, portable reputation, cryptographic proof of identity.

x402 Protocol

HTTP payment layer using 402 responses. Agents pay per-request without accounts or subscriptions.

Celo Network

Fast, low-cost blockchain with native USDC. Sub-cent fees make micropayments viable.
