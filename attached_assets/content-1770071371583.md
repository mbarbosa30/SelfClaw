Brand Policy

The Celo BrandKit includes resources to visually represent Celo and the mission of prosperity for all. The kit offers instructions and assets for community contributors to appropriately communicate Celo brand affiliation. By using the Celo brand consistently, we can connect in our shared purpose.

#### How to use this guide?

###### Important things to remember

The Celo Foundation is the owner of all the brand assets and derivatives. You may not:

- Register the trademark, domain, Celo Brand Asset, or derivative

- Modify Celo Brand Assets in any way such as by changing a design or color or adding words or elements


#### Brand Voice

Our tone of voice is how we consistently communicate with our audiences. We use simple sentences and approachable language to talk about complex topics.
